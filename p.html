BASI (SUGARCANE WINE)

Basi is Laoag local red wine.Also made from freshly squeezed organic sugarcane juice blended with the indigeneous Samak tree and fermented on earthen jars for years to give distinct taste and color.Like any other red wine Basi is essential to health concious people who value their life to the fullest.Taken moderately, surely one can enjoy its distinct flavor. Serve cool with "pulotan"what else can you ask for.
