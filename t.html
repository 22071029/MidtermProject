SINGKING BELL TOWER

The bell tower rests on sandy land. Its size weighing it down, the tower is believed to sink into the ground an inch every year.

SAINT WILLIAM CATHEDRAL

The current church was built in 1612 by Augusrinian fiars to replace wooden chapel.It serves as the seat or central church of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Laoag.
